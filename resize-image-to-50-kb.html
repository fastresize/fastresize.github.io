Resize Image to Exactly 50 KB or Under 50 KB

FastResize is a free online tool that helps you resize image to 50 KB or under 50 KB with high accuracy and minimal quality loss. This is especially useful for job application portals, government forms, website uploads, and email attachments where strict image size limits are enforced.

Simply upload your image and click resize. The tool automatically adjusts image dimensions and compression to meet the required 50 KB size while keeping the image clear and readable. All processing happens locally in your browser, so your images are never uploaded to any server.
