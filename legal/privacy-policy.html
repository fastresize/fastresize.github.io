Privacy Policy

At FastResize, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and image resizing tools.

Information We Do Not Collect

FastResize does not collect, store, or upload your images to any server. All image resizing and processing happens locally in your browser.

  • We do not store your images
  • We do not track image content
  • We do not require user accounts

Information We May Collect

We may collect limited non-personal information such as:

  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Anonymous usage statistics

This information is used only to improve website performance and user experience.

Cookies

FastResize may use basic cookies or similar technologies to ensure proper functionality of the website. These cookies do not store personal information and are not used for tracking individual users.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools such as analytics or advertising services. These services may collect anonymous usage data according to their own privacy policies. FastResize does not control how third-party services use this information.

Data Security

Since images are processed entirely in your browser and never uploaded, your data remains private and secure. We take reasonable steps to protect our website from unauthorized access or misuse.

Children’s Information

FastResize does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information on our website, please contact us so we can remove it.
